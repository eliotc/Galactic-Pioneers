Galactic Pioneers: Quick Start Guide

Destroy asteroids and score points in 30 seconds!

Controls:

  • Left Arrow: Rotate left
  • Right Arrow: Rotate right
  • Up Arrow: Thrust forward
  • Down Arrow: Reverse
  • Spacebar: Shoot

Gameplay:

  1. Shoot asteroids to score 100 points each.
  2. Avoid collisions - you have 3 lives.
  3. Game ends when time runs out or you lose all lives.

Tips:

  • Watch the red timer in the top right.
  • Maximum 7 bullets on screen at once.
  • Careful with thrust - slowing down takes time!
